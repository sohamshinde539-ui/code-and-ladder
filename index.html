Rules

  • Start at square 1. Roll 1–6 to move forward. You must roll exactly to land on 100.
  • Landing on the bottom of a ladder moves you up to its top.
  • Landing on a snake asks a coding question. Answer correctly to stay; answer wrong to slide down to the tail.
